About Me

I am Naga Kiran Reddy Karnati, a data scientist with over five years of experience developing and optimizing data and machine learning pipelines. My journey spans industries and continents—from banking and wealth management analytics in New York to research-oriented projects in India. Along the way I've built dashboards for leadership, designed generative AI solutions, and mentored teams to deliver high‑impact results.

I thrive at the intersection of data and storytelling, blending rigorous analysis with creative problem solving. My toolkit includes Python, R, SQL and a host of ML libraries like Scikit‑learn, TensorFlow and PyTorch. Whether it's training transformer networks on AWS SageMaker, reducing runtimes with efficient SQL, or experimenting with reinforcement learning algorithms, I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of what's possible with AI.

Outside of coding, I stay up to date with developments in generative AI and RAG methods, explore AR/VR technologies, and enjoy sharing my knowledge through mentorship and research collaborations.
